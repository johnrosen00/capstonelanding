Project

The Problem

Misinformation is a pressing global issue; it is increasingly easy to post false or misleading information on the internet and reach massive audiences.

Our Solution Approach

We created an online escape room to mimic the psychological and emotional responses elicited by real life interactions with digital misinformation. The aim of the escape room is to equip players with the necessary skills to identify misinformation online and become savvy, skeptical digital citizens.
